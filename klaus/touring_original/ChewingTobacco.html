The only correct way to chew chewing tobacco

What you need:
Chewing tobacco
Skoal, Copenhagen, Redman, King B, etc... - from now on called "dip"
A couple of beers
A cup
The right music
  1. Classical:
  2. Hardrock: Metallica - One
First, pack the dip by holding the can between the thumb and the middle finger of your preffered hand, laying your pointing finger on top of the can and shaking hard - like if you are trying to snap your fingers, so your pointing finger,that is lying on top of the can snaps to the can - be careful the can doesn't open while your doing this, or chewing tobacco will be spread all over the place.

Second, take a small amount of the dip (there should be about 20 - 39 portions in one can if I'm not mistaken) and put it your mouth. To be exactly: put it between your lower lips and teeth and hold it there until you spit it out again.

DO NOT SWALLOW ANYTHING. This is the most important rule. Don't swallow the dip, your spit or your tongue! NOTHING at all. Whatever is in your mouth and what you want to get rid of, spit it in the cup (if you put a bit water in the cup first, the result doesn't look as discusting and the cup will be easier to clean afterwards)

Now, press the juice out of the dip by squeezing your lips against your teeth - remember, don't swallow - if you got to much fluid in your mouth, spit it out.

Sit down, listen to ... first and afterwards to "One" (Metallica) and relax. Try to hold the dip inside at least until these two songs are over, but get rid of it if your beginning to feel bad. Believe me, you do not want to experience what is happening if you hold the dip too long! It's not nice and you'll feel sick as h... for a looong time.
