Motorcycle touring

In 1995 my brother and I traveled through the United States of America for half a year. 34.000 km later we feel like we have seen some of the most important places of this wonderful country and are pretty sure that we can provide a lot of helpful information to anybody who is planing a similar trip. We have tried to list what we think is important to bring with you, what sights are absolutely "must-sees", how the budget traveler can save money and more.
