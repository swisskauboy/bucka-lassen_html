Motorcycle Touring the USA

Klaus & Dirk Bucka-Lassen — March to September 1995

34,000 Kilometers
(21,000 mi for the
metrically challenged)
6 Months
2 Brothers
2 Motorcycles

In 1995 two Danish brothers set out on the adventure of a lifetime: half a year exploring the United States on motorcycles. 34,000 km later they had seen the Grand Canyon at sunrise, raced a Porsche on Highway 1, soaked in hot springs in Ouray, and made friends from coast to coast, all on a student budget. This site collects everything they learned along the way.
