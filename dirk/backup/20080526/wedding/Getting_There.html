From Copenhagen Airport (CPH)(www.cph.dk):

  1. 1.Take the train directly to Middelfart station. Travel time around 2h:15m. Check www.dsb.dk for your itinerary.

  2. 2.Take a cab from Middelfart station to Middelfart FerieCenter. Estimated travel time: 5-10 minutes. Estimated costs: 70 DKK (approximately $15)

  3. 3.You’re there!


Alternatively

You could consider flying into Hamburg (HAM), Germany or Billund (BLL), Denmark.
