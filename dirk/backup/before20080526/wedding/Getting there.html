Getting There
From Copenhagen Airport (www.cph.dk):
  1. 1.Take the train directly to Middelfart station. Estimated traveltime:  2h:16m. Check www.dsb.dk for your itinerary.
  2. 2.Take a cap from Middelfart station to Middelfart FerieCenter. Estimated traveltime: 5-10 minutes.
  3. 3.You’re there!
 
Alternatively, you could consider flying into Hamburg, Germany or Billund, Denmark.
